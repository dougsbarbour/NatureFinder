The Log Cabin

was originally built by Jesse Faris about 1800, three miles from this spot. The cabin later became the home of the family of Josiah Coltharp, a Confederate soldier.

Springfield

was built in 1806 by my ancestor, John Springs, III. He invested in land, railroads, and cotton. Today Springfield serves as headquarters of Leroy Springs & Company.

Nation Ford Road

also called "The Great Wagon Road", runs throught the Greenway property. It originated as an Indian Trading Path, but in the mid-1700s brought many Europeans to settle the Carolina back country. This path leads to the crossing, or "ford", of the Catawba River.
