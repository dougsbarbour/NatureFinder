The Dairy Barn

was built by my father in 1946 for dairy farming. the milk was sold in Charlotte, Fort Mill, and Rock Hill. Later beef cattle replaced the dairy cows. In 1996 it was renovated for parties and public events.

The Nature Center

This turn of the century farmhouse was built by Josiah Coltharp for his growing family. It was later the home of his married daughter, Fanny Gibson. This house was moved to the Greenway and now serves as the Nature Center.
